MAIN ROOM

In our main room, you'll find our beautifully lit bamboo bar and a grand communal table where families, friends, and strangers dine and celebrate together. It also contains curated museumworthy wall art designed by Dara Young, which recreates a Caribbean village, complete with miniature stores and people.

THE CHAMPAGNE ROOM

Enjoy the underground Champagne Room, which overflows with the colors of the Caribbean sea.

LOVERS ROCK

Upon entering Kokomo, you'll discover Lovers Rock, with bistro sealing and our customers' favorite hanging swings perfect for two.
